What We Do

Sanergy builds healthy, prosperous communities by making hygienic sanitation
affordable and accessible throughout Africa's informal settlements.


Sanitation

We build and lease low-cost, high quality sanitation facilities called Fresh Life Toilets

Collection

We collect the waste on a daily basis. Our Fresh Life teams are trained and equipped to operate safely.

Treatment

We convert the waste into useful end-products such as organic fertilizer and insect-based animal feed.

Re-Use

We then sell the organic fertilizer and high protein animal feed to a variety of Kenyan farmers.
